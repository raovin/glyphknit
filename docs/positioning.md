# GlyphKnit Solutions positioning note

## Narrative choices

- The site leads with Vin as a forward-deployed software delivery partner for product teams, not as a narrow QA contractor. The copy keeps release, testing, debugging, automation, and CI experience as credibility underneath a broader software delivery offer.
- Public project labels are anonymized. The 2024-2025 work is labeled as agency engagement work, and the 2023-2024 work is labeled as an independent software delivery project without a legal entity suffix.
- Client and employer names are not used on the public page. The case studies are described by delivery pattern, risk reduced, and hand-off.
- The hero focuses on specific work buyers need: internal tools, release automation, API/cloud integration checks, data validation, CI quality gates, and test infrastructure.
- AI is positioned as a tool used for scoped productivity gains, mainly test generation, case expansion, review support, and documentation. The copy avoids broad AI consulting claims.
- The public page keeps the 9 years of experience claim, but removes or softens the more specific employer-tied metrics until Vin approves them for public use.
- The footer uses "GlyphKnit Solutions" with neutral remote delivery wording. It does not claim an entity suffix, city, country, work authorization, or operating territory.
- The LinkedIn vanity link is removed until a confirmed public target is available.
